Logo and Identity

Canopy is a personal finance app that acts as a protective layer over your financial life. Just as a forest canopy captures light, regulates climate, and supports growth, Canopy transforms your spending into insight, keeps your finances balanced, and helps your goals take root over time. The logo reflects this directly — a grounded foundation held under two leaves forming a roof, representing protection, clarity, and the steady growth happening underneath.
