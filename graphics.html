Graphics

Canopy's graphics use watercolor hand-drawn illustration to bring the gamification system to life. Users progress through five quest levels tied to their spending habits and financial goals. Reaching level five unlocks a unique colored tree, randomized as a reward, which is then stored in their personal garden. The watercolor style was chosen because it feels organic and alive — much like the process of building healthy financial habits itself.

Unlockable Trees — Reward Collection

Upon reaching Level 5, users unlock one randomly assigned watercolor tree, which is added to their personal garden. Each tree has a unique species and rarity. The collection encourages continued engagement — there's always something new to discover.
