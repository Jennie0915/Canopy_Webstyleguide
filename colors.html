Color

Canopy's palette is drawn from the forest floor to the canopy ceiling. Each color has a defined role — from the deep stability of pine and teal to the light warmth of lemon chiffon. Together, they create a system that feels grounded, natural, and full of life.

Pine Teal
#004738
R 0 G 71 B 56 HSL 165° 100% 14%

The foundation of the brand. Used for navbars, primary headings, and strong UI elements. Evokes the stability of deep forest roots.

#2E8D6F
Jungle Teal
#2E8D6F
R 46 G 141 B 111 HSL 161° 51% 37%

The primary interactive color. Used for buttons, links, icons, and highlighted UI elements. Rich but approachable.

#8DAB59
Muted Green
#8DAB59
R 141 G 171 B 89 HSL 88° 32% 51%

Used for accent text, chart fills, and secondary visual signals. Midpoint between forest and foliage — earthy and optimistic.

#CCEA9D
Tea Green
#CCEA9D
R 204 G 234 B 157 HSL 88° 62% 77%

Light fill for badges, tags, and positive states. Represents new growth and budding potential — gentle and encouraging.

#EFC71D
Bright Ember
#EFC71D
R 239 G 199 B 29 HSL 49° 86% 53%

The accent and attention color. Used for callouts, alerts, and financial highlights. A warm sunlight that cuts through the canopy.

#FFFBD3
Lemon Chiffon
#FFFBD3
R 255 G 251 B 211 HSL 52° 100% 91%

The softest tone in the system. Used for page backgrounds, card fills, and logo on dark surfaces. Warm, airy, and calm.
