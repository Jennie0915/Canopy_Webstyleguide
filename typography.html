DM Serif Display is a transitional serif with high-contrast strokes and subtly organic curves, giving it an editorial warmth that feels grounded rather than corporate. At headline sizes, it carries a natural, living quality that mirrors Canopy’s growth theme without sacrificing polish. Paired with DM Sans — its purpose-built companion — the two share the same proportions and rhythm, making the combination feel cohesive and considered.

DM Sans

Body, UI Colophon Foundry Google Fonts Free

A geometric sans-serif designed as DM Serif Display's companion. Used for all body text, UI labels, and data — clean, legible, and trustworthy at every size.
